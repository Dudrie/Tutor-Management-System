Presentations

Roles:Tutor

To change the presentations a student has made you have to follow step (1) to (3):

  1. Sheet Selection: Select the sheet the student has presented a solution for.

  2. Presentation Points: Enter the amount of presentation points the student has gained for the selected sheet.

    note

    You can enter the presentation points for multiple students at once and only save one time in the end.

  3. Save: Save the changes.

  4. Total points: Displays the total points a student has throughout all sheets.

  5. Abort: Resets form to the last saved state.
